    • How to use TASERS

    The electric taser device is a very quick way to defend yourself against an attacker. Some tips to keep in mind when using a taser are:

  • Press the button to activate the taser and aim it at your target.
  • Make sure to practice your aim and grip on the taser, so you know the movements to take in the event of an attack, and so you don't fumble and waste time.
  • Tasers are battery-powered, so make sure it always has working batteries in them.
  • Make sure you asre putting it in an easily-accessible area so you can grab it quickly.

    • How to use PEPPER SPRAY

    Pepper spray, or mace, is a very quick way to subdue an attacker that allows range between you and ther attacker. Some tips to keep in mind when using pepper spray are:

  • If you are walking into a dark or isolated place, already have the pepper spray in hand. This allows for a quicker response. If not, keep it accessible.
  • The most effective range is from 3 to 10 feet depending on if the pepper spray emits a stream or a mist.
  • They cannot be carried into certain buildings, so do your research on whether or not it is permitted.
